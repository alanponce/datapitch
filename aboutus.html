Data Pitch

Data Driven Innovation

In a networked economy like the European one, most great challenges organisations face are best mastered through a well-balanced combination of collaboration and competition that includes things like sharing data in a secure, responsible way; working together with startups to find the most talented people to solve a given problem; and contributing to industry groups to reduce transaction costs through technology standardisation.

DataPitch will provide the technological and legal infrastructure for contributors to share their data in a secure and reliable environment for SMEs to experiment with, and accompaniment to the most promising SMEs, in the form of mentorship, networking and opportunities to present their work in top-notch forums and conferences.

DataPitch also will strengthen the European data economy by setting up a cross-sector, European Data Innovation Lab in which startups and SMEs can explore and develop new ideas to real-world challenges using data.
